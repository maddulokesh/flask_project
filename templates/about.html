About

The project is an overview for the management of the agricultural sector. This website helps the farmers to implement the profits for their growth and this reduces the work of the farmers. Our project provides the data about the essential availability of seeds, irrigation facilities, harvesting machineries and also seasonal crops. On the research of our project we found that 60% of the farmers had smart phones which sort out the problem and for the other remaining farmers there will be a provision of volunteers. Our main moto behind this project is that it helps the future India to meet their needs for the hunger.
